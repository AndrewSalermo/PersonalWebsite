My Relationship with Social Media and Mental Health

Posted on [4/1/24]

When choosing a topic for this infographic, I aimed to focus on a subject that I could resonate with. In 2023, I realized I had been wasting too much time on apps such as YouTube, TikTok, and Instagram. When checking at my screen time, I saw I was spending hours on each platform daily. While social media is a powerful tool where you can connect with others and learn new things, 90% of my time was spent on DOOM Scrolling, especially before bed. This would cut into my personal time, work life, and even had begun affecting my sleep. I knew I was beginning to become addicted to social media and decided to limit my screen time to an hour each day. After making this change, I became more productive and incorporated better habits into my lifestyle. One of my values in life is to strive to become a better version of myself every day and I hope this infographic inspires someone to better their own social media habits.
